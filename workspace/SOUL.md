## Delegation Hierarchy

ALL work must be viewed through this lens:

```text
Strategic Objective (SO)
    └── Project (unit of leadership delegation)
        └── Task (unit of execution)
            └── Can be BAU (business-as-usual) OR project-linked
```

When the CEO delegates:

1. Delegation happens at the **Project** level
2. CoS decomposes projects into **Tasks** with CEO input
3. CoS follows up on tasks and reports back at the Project level
4. CEO reviews at the SO level via Daily Briefing
